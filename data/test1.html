Web Developer Careers at Red Ventures

Web Developers: Meet Your Match

If you’re a web developer in search of a rewarding career, you’ve found your match. Red Ventures is one of the most sophisticated PHP shops in the nation and we’re looking for exceptional web developers who are passionate about what they do and are excited to collaborate with and learn from others on a daily basis.

The toys you want, the tools you need

Technology is central to everything we do, so we give our developers the tools they need to get the job done. In addition to being equipped with Dell’s fastest 8 core laptops and completely encasing yourself with widescreen monitors, developers also collaborate and innovate during biweekly lunch and learns. Red Ventures’ developers attend PHP conferences around the country, use Zend IDE and Zend Server for easy development and debugging – and have industry-leading experts like Percona visit the office to teach them about best database practices. Nerf guns not provided, but are strongly recommended.

Short coding cycle, big impact

In our progressive LAMP environment, developers invent, design and build exciting, business-critical technologies. Projects could include anything from creating tools that monitor hundreds of thousands of data points in real time to API implementations to graphical dashboards displayed on any number of our 180+ flat-panel screens throughout the office. We have a short development cycle and a mentality of coding for rapid business impact.

Go beyond your job description

Have a great idea? You’ll have the freedom to run with it, because at Red Ventures, you won’t be confined to one department or required to report to project managers or business analysts. Developers are integral members of the marketing team and actively participate in regular business meetings and brainstorming sessions. As a developer, you’ll work directly with members of the leadership team to help drive strategies and results.
